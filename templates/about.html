Who We Are

PetAdopt is a non-profit initiative focused on connecting loving families with rescued and homeless animals across India. Our work is driven by compassion, community, and care.

Our Mission

To rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome abandoned pets, giving them a second chance at life. We believe every pet deserves a family that cares.

Our Vision

We envision a society where every animal has a safe place to live, and adoptions replace pet store purchases to stop the cycle of breeding abuse.
